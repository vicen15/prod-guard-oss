<h1>Pricing</h1>

<p>
prod-guard is licensed using a <strong>simple, offline-friendly model</strong>
designed for professional and enterprise environments.
</p>

<p>
No subscriptions tied to runtime metrics.
No outbound calls.
No vendor lock-in.
</p>

<hr/>

<h2>Pricing Philosophy</h2>

<p>
prod-guard is priced according to <strong>value delivered</strong>, not usage noise.
</p>

<p>
It prevents:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Production incidents</li>
  <li>Emergency rollbacks</li>
  <li>Security exposure</li>
  <li>Compliance violations</li>
</ul>

<p>
The cost of a single avoided incident typically exceeds the annual license cost.
</p>

<hr/>

<h2>License Model</h2>

<p>
prod-guard uses <strong>signed license files</strong>:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Validated at startup</li>
  <li>No network dependency</li>
  <li>Works in air-gapped environments</li>
</ul>

<p>
Licenses are issued per organization or individual and can cover multiple services.
</p>

<hr/>

<h2>Plans</h2>

<h3>FREE</h3>

<p>
For evaluation, learning, and basic production safety.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Core production checks</li>
  <li>Non-blocking warnings</li>
  <li>Community-supported</li>
</ul>

<p>
<strong>Price:</strong> Free
</p>

<hr/>

<h3>PRO (Premium)</h3>

<p>
For professional teams running real production workloads.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>All FREE features</li>
  <li>Full premium check set</li>
  <li>Security & hardening checks</li>
  <li>License expiration warnings</li>
  <li>Priority issue handling</li>
</ul>

<p>
<strong>Typical use:</strong>
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Consultants</li>
  <li>Small teams</li>
  <li>Independent vendors</li>
</ul>

<p>
<strong>Price:</strong> €1200
</p>

<hr/>

<h2>What You Are Paying For</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Hard-earned production knowledge</li>
  <li>Curated, opinionated checks</li>
  <li>Ongoing maintenance and updates</li>
  <li>Compatibility with new Spring Boot versions</li>
</ul>

<p>
You are not paying for:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Hosting</li>
  <li>Telemetry</li>
  <li>Data collection</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>Renewal & Expiration</h2>

<p>
Licenses are time-bound.
</p>

<p>
prod-guard will:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Warn before expiration</li>
  <li>Gracefully downgrade to FREE mode after expiration</li>
</ul>

<p>
No service interruption. No surprises.
</p>

<hr/>

<h2>Why Not Usage-Based Pricing?</h2>

<p>
Usage-based pricing penalizes:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Growth</li>
  <li>High availability</li>
  <li>Good engineering practices</li>
</ul>

<p>
prod-guard pricing is:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Predictable</li>
  <li>Transparent</li>
  <li>Procurement-friendly</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>Getting a License</h2>

<p>
To request a license:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Provide licensee name</li>
  <li>Choose validity period</li>
</ul>

<p>
You will receive a signed license file usable immediately.
</p>
